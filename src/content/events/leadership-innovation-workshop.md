## About This Event

The Leadership Innovation Workshop is a hands-on, half-day experience designed for leaders who want to unlock their team's creative potential. Through guided exercises and real-world scenarios, participants will develop practical skills for fostering innovation.

## Workshop Modules

### Module 1: Innovation Mindset
Discover the principles behind innovative thinking and learn how to cultivate a culture of experimentation within your team.

### Module 2: Team Dynamics
Explore the science of high-performing teams and learn techniques for building trust, encouraging diverse perspectives, and managing creative conflict.

### Module 3: From Idea to Execution
Learn a practical framework for taking innovative ideas from concept to implementation, including prioritization, prototyping, and iteration.

## What to Bring

- An open mind and willingness to participate
- Current challenges or projects you'd like to apply new thinking to
- Business cards for networking

## Facilitator

This workshop is led by experienced leadership coaches with backgrounds in organizational psychology and innovation management.
